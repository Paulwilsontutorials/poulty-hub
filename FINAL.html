Hatchery Management

Hatchery management involves the careful planning and operation of facilities where eggs are incubated and hatched, ensuring high hatch rates and healthy offspring. Key aspects include maintaining proper temperature, humidity, and ventilation in incubators; handling and storing eggs correctly; practicing strict hygiene and biosecurity; and providing appropriate post-hatch care such as sorting, vaccination, and brooding. Efficient record-keeping and performance monitoring are also essential to identify issues and improve productivity in poultry.
